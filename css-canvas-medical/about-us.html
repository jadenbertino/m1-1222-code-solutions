Clinicians should have software they love.

Our mission is to give clinicians and their teams highly usable, intelligent toosl to advance their practice of medicine — and improve their day-to-day.

What We Believe

Why did we enter this crowded field? Because medical records have fallen dramatically short of their promise to improve the quality and efficiency of health care. Clinicians spend far too long at their computers. Work-Flows are overly restrictive. Valuable medical data is left unstructured.

We are honored, and humbled, when a practice chooses or as their technology partner. Medicine is a delicate craft that mixes art and science —technology can help or hinder this craft. Working with clinicians across the country, we think we have begun to build something that helps.
